About Neon Ink

Neon Ink brings bold creativity and technical precision together to create tattoos that last—and stand out.

Our artists specialize in Blackwork, Fine Line, Realism, and custom symbolic design. Whether you arrive with a clear vision or just a spark of inspiration, we collaborate closely to turn ideas into art.

We focus on clean technique, a safe environment, and a professional experience from consultation to aftercare.
