About Neon Ink

Neon Ink brings bold creativity and technical precision together to create tattoos that last—and stand out.

Our artists specialize in Blackwork, Fine Line, Realism, and custom symbolic design. Whether you arrive with a clear vision or just a spark of inspiration, we collaborate closely to turn ideas into art.

We focus on clean technique, a safe environment, and a professional experience from consultation to aftercare.

Alex “Raven” Carter

Blackwork • Fine Line • Custom Symbolism

Known for bold contrast and razor-clean precision, Raven brings a unique geometric and symbolic style to every piece. His work blends modern minimalism with ancient visual languages, making him one of the studio’s most requested artists.

  • Experience: 8+ years
  • Focus: Blackwork, symbolism, fine line

Maya Torres

Traditional • Japanese Irezumi

Maya specializes in rich cultural motifs and timeless tattoo traditions. Her pieces incorporate flowing movement, narrative imagery, and a deep respect for the origins of Japanese and American traditional ink.

  • Experience: 6+ years
  • Focus: Blackwork, symbolism, fine line

Leo Maddox

Realism • Portrait Work

Leo’s realism work captures lifelike detail through soft shading, delicate gradients, and a deep focus on human expression. Whether portraits or wildlife, his tattoos feel alive on the skin.

  • Experience: 7+ years
  • Focus: Portrait realism

Jordan Hale

Blackwork • Fine Line • Custom Symbolism

Jordan blends bold modern styles with expressive, painterly shading. Their work stands out through thoughtful composition, emotional depth, and smooth transitions that make every piece feel dynamic.

  • Experience: 5+ years
  • Focus: Neo-traditional & expressive linework

Rowan Ellis

Custom Linework • Script • Minimalism

Rowan specializes in personalized designs—script, symbols, minimalist pieces, and custom micro-work. Their tattoos emphasize clarity, balance, and storytelling through simplicity.

  • Experience: 4+ years
  • Focus: Script & minimal custom work
